Seblak

You're Not yet in Bandung if you haven't tried eating seblak. This typical Bandung food made from wet crackers has a distinctive taste because it is made from spices and has fillings such as vegetables, meat and eggs.

There are several great places to taste seblak, but one worth recommending is the Seblak Jebred which has extra claws in the filling, plus crispy tofu, sausage and gravel. To make it even more delicious, increase the spicy version, OK! The price is also quite pocket friendly, you know!

Es Goyobod

Starting to heat up with the unfriendly Bandung air? Relieve it by enjoying a serving of refreshing goyobod ice. This Sundanese cold drink is indeed the right "medicine" to overcome your thirst.

Es Goyobod Kliningan contains chewy sago palm dough with coconut milk, sweetened condensed milk and ice shavings. Complementary materials range from avocado, plain bread, peuyeum, Chinese henna, fro, and coconut. With a very affordable price, guaranteed you will add and add more!

Cuanki

The dish is similar to Malang meatballs. In one portion of cuanki consists of aci, dumplings, meatballs, boiled dumplings, fried dumplings and fried meatballs. The difference is, the noodles used are not yellow noodles. With the addition of celery and spring onions, Cuanki is also more savory and clear.

Many places to be able to enjoy cuanki, considering that initially this culinary was offered by the seller by walking around. But now there is one spot that is quite popular for culinary lovers, namely Cuanki Serayu, which has been around since 1990.

Gehu Jeletot

For those looking for a spicy taste, Gehu Jeletot's culinary delights can be the right alternative. This new snack in the city of Bandung is widely hunted for its spicy, savory and pleasant taste. In addition to the vegetable topping in the dish, such as sliced carrots and cabbage, there are also beef slices to add flavor.

Gehu Hot Jeletot is the right place for those of you who want this special Bandung food. The price is relatively more expensive than other similar products in Bandung, but the taste quality is quite different.
